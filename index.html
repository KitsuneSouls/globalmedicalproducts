About

In 2006, we saw a need for low cost home medical equipment and responded with HME supplies from major manufacturers such as Omron and Mabis, as well as rapidly growing companies such as Allison Medical and Retractable Technologies, Inc. We offer fast order shipment and delivery to you as well as worry free drop shipments direct to your customers. We are committed to offering the best pricing in the industry so that our customers can realize their profitability and growth potentials. Our company and sales staff are dedicated to represent our manufacturers with integrity while ensuring superior sales results. We make it our business to collaborate with manufacturers to reach regional and independent suppliers nationwide and create demand for your product.
